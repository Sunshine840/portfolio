About

The engineer who thinks
like a founder.

I'm Sanjeev Kumar — systems engineer and technology founder based in New Zealand. MSc Software Engineering. BEng Electrical & Electronics. Built and shipped at scale across Europe and New Zealand.

I think from hardware up. That's how you find the cost savings, the bottlenecks, and the architectural decisions that actually matter — before they become expensive problems.

I don't just ship features. I ask why something costs what it costs, and what it would take to build it right the first time. And if the answer requires learning something new — that's not a barrier, that's the job.
